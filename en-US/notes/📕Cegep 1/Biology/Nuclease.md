# Nuclease

[[Enzyme]] that cleaves [[Nucleic Acid|nucleic acids]] by hydrolyzing phosphodiester bonds between nucleotides

## Types

### Exonuclease

Degrades *from the end* of the nucleotide chain

### Endonuclease

Cleaves *in the middle* of the nucleotide chain
