# Mean

Sum of measurements divided by the number of measurements
Measure of [[central tendency]]

For classless data:

$$
\overline{x} = \frac{\sum_{ i = 1 }^{ n } x_{i}}{n}
$$

For data grouped in classes:

$$
\overline{x} = \frac{\sum_{ i = 1 }^{ n } n_ic_i}{n}
$$
