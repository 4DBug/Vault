# Phenotype

`Coll.` observable characteristics of an organism

Influenced by:

- Dominance relationship of [[Allele|alleles]] in the [[genotype]]
- Environment
	- Food supply
	- Behaviour of the parents

Includes:

- Behaviour
- Appearance
- Biochemical properties

> Phenotype most commonly observed in nature
