# Cytoskeleton

Dynamic network of [[protein]] fibres in the [[cytoplasm]]

## Types

### Microtubule

Thickest fibre constructed from *tubulin*
Hollow rod

Functions:

- Maintain cell shape
- 

> [!abstract] Tubulin
> Globular protein

### Microfilament

Thinnest fibre constructed from *actin*
Solid rod

Functions:

- Maintains cell shape
- Muscle contraction
- Cell mobility
- Cell division
- Cytoplasmic streaming
