# Hydrosolubility

> [!abstract] Hydrophobic
> *Insoluble* in water
> 
> Characterized by lots of *hydrocarbon* ($H$ &  $C$)

> [!abstract] Hydrophilic
> *Very soluble* in water
> 
> Characterized by lots of *oxygen*
> 
> Charged

> [!abstract] Amphipathic
> Has both hydrophobic and hydrophilic regions
