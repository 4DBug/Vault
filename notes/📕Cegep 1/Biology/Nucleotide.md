# Nucleotide

[[Monomer]] of nucleic acids

Composed of:

- Nitrogenous (nitrogen-containing or N-) base
- Pentose sugar
- Phosphate

## Types of sugars

- Deoxyribose (DNA only)
- Ribose (RNA only)

## Types of N-bases

### Pyrimidine

*Single* ring

- Cytosine (`abbr.` C)
- Thymine (`abbr.` T) (DNA only)
- Uracil (`abbr.` U) (RNA only)

### Purine

Double* ring

- Adenine (`abbr.` A)
- Guanine (`abbr.` G)
