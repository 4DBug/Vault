# L'HÃ´pital's Rule

Suppose $f$ and $g$ are differentiable functions and that $\lim_{ x \to a } \frac{f(x)}{g(x)}$ evaluates to $\frac{0}{0}$ or $\frac{\infty}{\infty}$, then

$$
\lim_{ x \to a } \frac{f(x)}{g(x)} = \lim_{ x \to a } \frac{f'(x)}{g'(x)}
$$
