# Product Rule

Let $f$ and $g$ be two **differentiable** functions, then

$$
\frac{d}{dx}(f(x)\cdot g(x)) = f(x)g'(x) + g(x)f'(x)
$$

The rule extends to >2 functions:

$$
\frac{d}{dx}(f(x)g(x)h(x)) = f'(x)g(x)h(x) + g'(x)f(x)h(x) + h'(x)f(x)g(x)
$$
