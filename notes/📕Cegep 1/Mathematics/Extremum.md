# Extremum

Point $x = c$ of a function $f$ over its domain $I$ (**global**) / an interval $I$ (**local**) when $\forall x \in I$, $f(c) > f(x)$ (**maximum**) / $f(c) < f(x)$ (**minimum**)
`Pl.` extrema

Only occurs at critical points
