# Karma

Law of moral causation, concept in [[Hinduism]], [[Buddhism]], [[Jainism]] and [[Sikhism]]
Actions in the current life will influence the reincarnation
Good actions -> good karma -> rewarded
Bad actions -> bad karma -> punished
